Pneumonia Facts:

Pneumonia is an infection in the lungs that cause the air sacks to fill with liquids, or even puss Common Symptoms are; coughing phlegm or puss, fever, Chills, difficulty breathing Pneumonia can be caused from bacteria infection , as well as a viral infection, it is most commonly after a patient has had the flu or a cold Chronic Pneumonia is Classified as Pneumonia that has has been concurrent for the last 6 weeks, and is reoccurring Anyone can get pneumonia but it is most deadly for those above the age of 65 (which is 62 million people in the US) and those under the age of 2 due to their lung capacity being low
